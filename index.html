WHY INVEST IN PPF PROTECTION

We all love that flawless showroom shine of a brand new paint finish. But sadly, it does not last the life of a vehicle. When a vehicle's finish looks dull, scratched up and worn both pride and resale value are lost. Today's automotive industry has adapted new environmental friendly water based paints that are much softer and scratch easily. Thus, paint protection is needed now more than ever before.


PREVENTION IS BETTER THAN A CURE

Probably the most important aspect of owning a vehicle is how does it hold its resale value according to its condition after 3-4 years. Statistically a vehicle loses around 40% of its value over the first 3 years of its life. 9H-LDC PRO is a high quality durable paint protection that will lock in that showroom shine and absorb the damage that would normally be caused directly to the paint retaining its original shine and value longer.
